Key Features

Smart where it matters.

Every feature engineered for the way you actually live — reliable, connected, and always a step ahead.

In-Built Door Bell

One-touch video alert to your phone — see who's at the door even when you're not home.

Wi-Fi Connectivity

Remote app access from anywhere in the world. Check status, grant entry, monitor logs.

Auto Locking

Door secures itself automatically behind you. Never worry about forgetting to lock up.

Tamper Alarm

Instant intrusion alert — audible siren plus a push notification the moment your door is tampered with.

Emergency Power

USB backup port ensures you're never locked out — jump-start your lock with any power bank.
